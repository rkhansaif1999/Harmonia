FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything You Need to Know

Can't find what you're looking for? Our support team is always ready to help.

What is Harmonia?

+
Harmonia is a global digital work platform connecting contributors with businesses through opportunities like surveys, AI-related projects, data annotation, research participation, reviews, and testing.

Is creating an account free?

+
Absolutely — creating a Harmonia account is completely free.

Do I need previous experience to start?

+
Not always. Some opportunities are beginner-friendly, while others may require specific knowledge or skills. Each project clearly explains its own requirements before you begin.

Is Harmonia available worldwide?

+
Yes. Harmonia welcomes members from many countries. However, certain opportunities may depend on project requirements, regional availability, language, or client preferences.

How are payments processed?

+
Approved work becomes eligible for payment according to our Payment Policy. Payment schedules and available methods may vary depending on your country, project requirements, and verification status.

Who can create projects on Harmonia?

+
Businesses, organizations, researchers, startups, and other approved clients can use Harmonia to connect with contributors for a wide range of digital projects.

How does Harmonia maintain quality?

+
Depending on the project, submissions may undergo automated validation, manual review, client approval, or other quality assurance processes designed to deliver reliable results.

What happens if someone violates the rules?

+
Accounts that violate Harmonia's policies may be reviewed and, where appropriate, restricted or suspended to help protect the integrity of the community.

Can I have more than one account?

+
Unless explicitly permitted by Harmonia, each individual should maintain only one personal account. Multiple accounts may interfere with platform fairness and could result in account action.
